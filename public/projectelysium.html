ProjectElysium

About

First established in December 2022. ProjectElysium started as a one man graphic design and development team
taking commissions from international clients. After getting recognition from clients and fellow developers.
Project Elysium decided to expand into two teams which consists of a modeler, texture artist, animator, and developer
each with their own team leader. This allows them to work on tasks more efficiently, and allow them to improve on their specific
skillset.

ProjectElysium slowly gained its reputation as one of the go to studios for quality models that is budget friendly
After August 2023. The team of ProjectElysium decided to try on new things and expand their horizons
tackling game development using UnrealEngine 5 and sculpting assets using Zbrush.
