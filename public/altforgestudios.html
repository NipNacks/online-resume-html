Altforge Studios

About

Established in January 2023. Altforge Studios was created in the dreams of developing games using Unreal Engine 5.
From creating game ready assets, sculpting models, to coding mobs and functions. Altforge studios was put forth with
a big vision. To give the public a nostalgic game where they can sit back after a tiring day and play with their family and friends.

In August 2023. The team of Altforge Studios has temporarily stopped their development in order to purse each of their careers.
Each focusing on their specific courses which they can further better their individual portfolios and skills.
