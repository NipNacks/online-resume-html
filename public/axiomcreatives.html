About

Established in August 2024. AxiomCreatives was previously known as ProjectElysium and rebranded into what
it currently is today. A full re-staff of its team has been made only with its founding member and a new key
team member to expand it again to what it once was.

With its goals unchanged, AxiomCreatives desires to make a comeback in the next coming years and put forth again
its name as the premier go to studio for quality budget-friendly models. With its vision in mind, it also strives to expand
its horizons by also creating their website and social medias.
